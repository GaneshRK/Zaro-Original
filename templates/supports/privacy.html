ZARO – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22 December 2025

ZARO is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number (optional)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via Razorpay; ZARO does not store card or banking details
  • Other Data: Cookies, tracking pixels, and analytics data for performance and marketing

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide access to purchased digital products
  • To process payments securely via third-party gateways
  • To send updates, newsletters, and promotions via email or WhatsApp
  • To analyze website traffic and improve user experience

3. Marketing & Communications

  • Users may opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Promotional messages are sent only to users who opt in
  • Email tools may include platforms such as Mailchimp or Brevo

4. Cookies & Tracking

ZARO uses cookies and tracking technologies for:

  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Improving website functionality
  • Personalized content and advertising

Users can disable cookies via browser settings; however, some features of the website may not function properly.

5. Data Security

  • ZARO uses industry-standard security measures to protect user data
  • Payment transactions are handled only by trusted third-party gateways
  • We do not store sensitive financial information on our servers

6. Data Sharing

ZARO does not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

We may share data only with:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay, Stripe, PayPal)
  • Marketing platforms (Mailchimp, Brevo)
  • Legal or government authorities if required by law

7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of their account and personal data

Requests can be sent to: zarogroups@gmail.com

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

ZARO may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or website notice.
